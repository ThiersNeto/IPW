About the Project

The Digital Health Portal was created with the aim of providing accurate and up-to-date information on the use of digital technologies in healthcare. With the rapid advancement of technology, it is essential to understand how these innovations can benefit our health and well-being.

Need for the Portal

We live in a digital era where technology influences all aspects of our lives, including health. This portal aims to fill the information gap and provide useful resources so that everyone can make the most of the advantages of digital health.
